Participant in the Europeana Libraries Project

DOAJ participated in the Europeana Libraries project 2010-2012. DOAJ is included in the portal which was launched in Tartu, Estonia on June 27, 2012. Offering free online access to high quality full text content, plus excellent search tools, it enables researchers to find, use and re-use a vast range of materials with ease. The content of DOAJ will be even more visible and disseminated through this portal.
Please have a look at the portal.
A promotional video for the website can also be viewed on our YouTube channel.
