Please read our 5 FAQs before sending us feedback:
  1. I want to publish my article in your journal.
    DOAJ is NOT a journal or a publisher. Find the journal where you want to publish your article and ask them directly.
  2. I want to index my journal in DOAJ. What do I do?
    Have a look at our selection criteria. If your journal fulfils the criteria please complete the suggestion form.
  3. I have suggested my journal to DOAJ. How long time do I have to wait?
    We process suggestions as fast as possible. If you have not received an answer within 2 months, feel free to contact us.
  4. Please send me articles about X subject.
    We do not supply that service. Do a search or Browse by subject
  5. Please send me a list of journals on X subject.
    You can either harvest the metadata or download a csv file.
