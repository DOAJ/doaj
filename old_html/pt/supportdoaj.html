Support DOAJ Our sponsors DOAJ members

Thanks to funding over the years from the Open Society Institute, The National Library of Sweden, SPARC, SPARC Europe, Axiell, EBSCO, INASP and Swedish Library Association, we have been able to operate, extend and improve DOAJ. We are very grateful for the contributions from our sponsors.

However there is still much work to be done and we need your support to continue running and developing the DOAJ. There are two ways to support DOAJ:

Membership

For those who want to support the DOAJ with an annual fixed contribution. This option has several benefits:

  • Acknowledgement and your logo on our DOAJ Membership Page
  • Subscription to e-mail list for DOAJ members
  • Access to the list of recently added titles
  • Access to the list of removed titles
  • The right to use DOAJ membership and logo in marketing activities

If you are interested go to [BECOME A MEMBER] where you will find more detail. We can now take payment via PayPal.

Become a Supporter

We recognise that not everyone can commit to an annual fixed contribution so you have the option to [BECOME A SUPPORTER] with whatever contribution you might find appropriate.
