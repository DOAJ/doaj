Reapplications

If you have 10 or fewer journals in DOAJ, you will need to log into your Publisher's Area and process your reapplications online. We have created two documents that you must read before you start:

  1. Some important notes on how to: fill in your reapplications online; submit them once you are finished; and come back to them if you need to make changes.
  2. We created a spreadsheet version of the Reapplication form with some important help notes. You can't edit this online but you can download a copy to your desktop.

If you have more than 10 journals, you will need to log into your Publishers Area and download the CSV file that we have created for you. We have created two documents that you must read before you start:

  1. Here are some important notes on how to: download your CSV; convert it into another format for easier completion; fill it in; reupload it to our servers.
  2. We created a version of the Reapplication form as a spreadsheet with some important help notes.

Creating DOAJ XML

For help with creating DOAJ XML and details on XML tags, read the File Upload Info section on our Features pages and the Uploading content section on our 'For Publishers' page.

Problems with XML files

For problems with XML file uploads, read the What to do if your XML upload fails and Troubleshooting your XML upload sections on our 'For Publishers' page.

Correcting articles on DOAJ

If you need to correct an article on DOAJ, upload it to us again. Read How to correct an error in Article Metadata? on our 'For Publishers' page.

Deleting articles from DOAJ

If an article or a range of articles needs to be deleted, please Contact Us. Please email us ALL of the following information:

  1. Name of the journal, as it appears in DOAJ
  2. The URL of the journal in DOAJ ( which will look something like http://doaj.org/toc/1234-5678 )
  3. The title of EVERY article to be deleted. If an article appears twice, include details of what is wrong so we know which one to delete.
  4. If you are asking us to delete a large range of content, there is no need to send the title of every article but you must include the volume and issue range. For example, Volume 1, Issue 1, Jan 2010 to Volume 2, Issue 4, Dec 2011.
  5. Send a screenshot if it will help.
