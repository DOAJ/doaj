Volunteering for DOAJ

DOAJ would not be able to do the work that it does without the help of its volunteers. We are grateful for the hours that all our volunteers contribute by reviewing new applications. We send our volunteers a bimonthly newsletter in order to share updates and news from the managing team; editorial group performance updates; industry updates and upcoming events; and we highlight tools to help them in their role.
