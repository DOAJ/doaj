Become a member or make a donation

DOAJ Membership is recommended for those who can support the DOAJ with an annual fixed contribution (annual automatic payment). For those who wish to make a longer-term, more sustainable and more substantial impact on the future of DOAJ, then please consider becoming part of the SCOSS coalition. All members are listed here on the DOAJ site and can use their DOAJ membership status and logo for marketing activities.

Your institution may also be interested in becoming a Sponsor. For more information email dom[at]doaj[.]org

If you publish or own an Open Access journal, you may prefer to become a Publisher Member. You will be listed on our Publisher Members page .

How to become a DOAJ member

This option is for members who can support DOAJ with an annual fixed contribution or the larger payment recommended by SCOSS. The fastest and most secure way to pay is via PayPal. You may also do a bank transfer. Please note that, due to restrictions at PayPal, we can only take payment in GBP, EUR and USD. For all payments over £5500 please email lars[at]doaj[.]org.

The rates for 2020 are:
  • Academic Libraries, Universities, Research Centers - £540 (€585 / $675) per year
  • Library Consortia, Library Associations, up to 40 members - £5400 (€5850 / $6750) per year
  • Library Consortia, Library Associations, up to 80 members - £10,800 (€11,700 / $13500) per year
  • Library Consortia, Library Associations, 80+ members: request a quote
