Journal Application Form

If you know a journal that should be included in DOAJ, please ask its publisher to complete this form.

We provide some guidance on the information we are looking for but that list is by no means exhaustive. Applicants are simply encouraged to complete the form clearly and honestly. If you do not know the answer to a question, you can request help by contacting us.

Currently, it is not possible to save your progress or for us to send you a confirmation email. We have created a spreadsheet version of the form and some other tips to help you. Guides to completing the form are available in other languages.
